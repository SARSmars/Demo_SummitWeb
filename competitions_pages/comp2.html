Decrypt is the good old case study competition. It is all about reading the situation and taking decisions – sometimes obvious and sometimes challenging. Guidelines

  • Team Size of 2-4 members with inter-college teams are allowed.
  • The top 10 teams will be shortlisted depending on their performance.
  • Teams will then present their deck to a panel of judges, and they will evaluate them.
  • The presentation can be 6 slides long including first and last slide.
  • Participants will be judged based on their depth of analysis, recommendations, and presentation quality
